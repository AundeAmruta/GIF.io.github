EMOJIES INFO

  • The first set of Emojis were originally created in 1999 by Japanese artist Shigetaka Kurita and included icons for the weather, traffic, technology and time.
  • There are 3,019 official emojis. The Unicode Consortium approves the new emojis.They released 230 new emojis in February 2019, which include the waffle.
  • Emojis are a unique approach to modern language. These digital icons are somewhat akin to modern hieroglyphics that edge out the need for words.
